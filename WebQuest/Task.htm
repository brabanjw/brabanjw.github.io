Through history, mathematics has played an important role in life.  

It is your task to find the important role math has played in art.  Find a piece of art influenced by math and write what it means to you and how it relates to mathematics.

It will also be your role to make mathematics more personable by writing two biographies of mathematicians.  Too often the text books are filled with Western Europeans, and there are contributions from other peoples that do not get covered in textbooks.  One of the biographies must be either about a woman or a person of color.

Finally, you will need to write about the role math plays in life.  This will be done by writing an essay on why it is important to learn math and how math relates to your life.

Keep in mind that you are Math’s spokesperson. Your papers and work will help people realize Math’s importance on the world. Good luck!
