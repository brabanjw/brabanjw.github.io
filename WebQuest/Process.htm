Biographies

Write a biography of two people that have made contributions to mathematics. At least one of these mathematicians must either be a woman or a person of color. The other mathematician can be any mathematician of your choice.

The biographies should answer the following questions:
• Date or period in which the person lived?
• A description of their life or where they lived?
• What were their contributions to Mathematics?

• Who made the work of art?
• Why did you choose this work of art?

• What are the reasons for learning math?
• How can math be applied to the real world?
• What were you own personal feelings towards math before and after the Math Public Relations Webquest?
