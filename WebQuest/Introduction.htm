There has been a separation over the years between people that dislike math and people that like math.  There are a number of people that think that they will never use the Math that is taught in schools.  The common phrase amongst people that dislike math is that "why are we doing this?"

 
Math Public Relations is a way of reaching out to the people that dislike math and question the reason for learning math.  It addresses the issue through art, history, and recent ideas why it is important to learn math.  


Prepare yourself, for you have been nominated Math's spokesperson in order to open the minds of your fellow students to the Mathematical possibilities.
